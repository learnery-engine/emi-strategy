# EMI Competitive Landscape

## Direct Competitors in Vietnam EMI Space

### ELSA Speak
- **Focus**: Pronunciation and speaking practice
- **Strength**: Strong brand recognition, mentioned by name in HCMC De An
- **Weakness**: Consumer-only, no B2B school platform, no CLIL content, no curriculum alignment
- **Threat level**: Low — complementary, not competing

### Grammarly
- **Focus**: Writing correction
- **Strength**: Also mentioned by name in HCMC De An
- **Weakness**: No Vietnamese context, no curriculum alignment, no school platform
- **Threat level**: Low — complementary tool

### Cambridge/IB/A-Level Programs
- **Focus**: International curriculum programs
- **Strength**: Gold-standard brand recognition
- **Weakness**: Extremely expensive, only for elite schools, not aligned to GDPT 2018
- **Threat level**: Medium in high-end segment; irrelevant for mass market

### Vietnamese EdTech (Manabie, HOCMAI, Topica)
- **Focus**: General tutoring / test prep
- **Weakness**: No EMI-specific product, no CLIL methodology, no government certification
- **Threat level**: Medium if they pivot

### International CLIL Platforms
- **Focus**: CLIL methodology tools (primarily European)
- **Weakness**: No Vietnamese localization, no government relationships, no curriculum alignment
- **Threat level**: Low

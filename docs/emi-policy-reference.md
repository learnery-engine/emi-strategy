# EMI Policy Reference — Verified Sources

## Status of the 3-Level Framework

**Important finding:** QD 2371/QD-TTg defines 7 evaluation dimensions and establishes percentage targets for schools reaching each level, but **does not define the specific criteria for Level 1, Level 2, and Level 3.** The decision mandates MOET to issue detailed assessment regulations separately (Article 1, Section III.2(c): "Xây dựng quy định về đánh giá chất lượng").

As of April 2026, the detailed level criteria are **pending issuance by MOET**. This is an opportunity — Learneris's EMI Readiness Assessment tool could become the de facto standard before MOET publishes their own.

### 7 Evaluation Dimensions

Schools are assessed across these 7 areas to determine their EMI level (direct from QD 2371 footnote):

| # | Dimension (Vietnamese) | Dimension (English) |
|---|----------------------|---------------------|
| 1 | Môi trường ngôn ngữ và truyền thông | Language environment and communication |
| 2 | Chương trình giáo dục và hoạt động dạy học | Educational programs and teaching activities |
| 3 | Học liệu, tài liệu | Learning materials and resources |
| 4 | Chuyển đổi số, ứng dụng trí tuệ nhân tạo và cơ sở vật chất | Digital transformation, AI application, and physical infrastructure |
| 5 | Đội ngũ cán bộ quản lý giáo dục, nhà giáo và người học | Management staff, teachers, and learners |
| 6 | Hợp tác quốc tế | International cooperation |
| 7 | Nghiên cứu khoa học và công nghệ | Scientific research and technology |

### What We Know About Each Level (from context in QD 2371 and HCMC De An)

| Level | Inferred Meaning (from policy context) | Source |
|-------|---------------------------------------|--------|
| **Mức độ 1** | English environment established — signage, announcements, activities in English; basic CLIL attempted; teachers have minimum proficiency | HCMC De An Section II, referencing "bước đầu đưa tiếng Anh thành ngôn ngữ thứ hai" and "mức độ 1" targets |
| **Mức độ 2** | Bilingual teaching active — some subjects taught in English (CLIL); teachers proficient at B2+; digital resources in place; international partnerships | HCMC De An targets show this as a mid-stage goal, achieved by advanced/quality schools first |
| **Mức độ 3** | Full EMI — English used naturally across teaching, management, and student life; aligned to international standards; research conducted in English | HCMC De An Phase 3 (2040-2045) vision: "Tiếng Anh được sử dụng một cách tự nhiên, phổ biến" |

> **Caveat:** These are inferred from context. The official detailed criteria for each level have not yet been published by MOET.

### 8 Task Categories (Nhiệm vụ và Giải pháp)

From QD 2371 Section III:

1. **Nâng cao nhận thức** — Raise societal awareness of English's role through strategic communication
2. **Xây dựng thể chế** — Build institutional frameworks: standards, evaluation criteria, and support mechanisms
3. **Phát triển đội ngũ** — Develop teaching workforce: training, proficiency assessment, recruitment incentives
4. **Phát triển chương trình & học liệu** — Develop curriculum and learning materials, including online platforms
5. **Đổi mới phương pháp** — Reform teaching methods: communicative approach, 4-skill integration, international assessment alignment
6. **Ứng dụng CNTT & AI** — Apply technology and AI: personalized learning platforms, digital infrastructure, teacher tech training
7. **Hợp tác quốc tế** — Strengthen international cooperation: bilateral agreements, foreign expert recruitment, knowledge transfer
8. **Phong trào thi đua** — Launch recognition programs for outstanding implementations

### Implementation Timeline (3 Phases)

| Phase | Period | Focus |
|-------|--------|-------|
| 1 | 2025-2030 | Foundation and standardization — build environment, pilot models, standardize programs |
| 2 | 2030-2040 | Expansion — scale EMI across schools, form English ecosystem |
| 3 | 2040-2045 | Completion — English used naturally throughout school life |

**Source:** HCMC De An Section II.3

### 4-Step Implementation Model (HCMC-specific)

1. **Nâng cao chất lượng** — Improve teaching quality, standardize programs against international benchmarks
2. **Mở rộng môi trường** — Expand English usage beyond classroom (clubs, exchanges, events)
3. **Ứng dụng công nghệ** — Apply technology: online platforms, bilingual content, AI integration
4. **Thí điểm & nhân rộng** — Pilot and scale: start with quality schools, evaluate, replicate

**Source:** HCMC De An Section II.4

## Learneris Strategic Implications

### Key Opportunity: Level Criteria Are Undefined
MOET has not yet published detailed criteria for Level 1/2/3. Whoever provides a practical assessment framework first becomes the de facto standard. Learneris should:
1. Build EMI Readiness Assessment tool aligned to the 7 dimensions
2. Propose scoring rubrics per dimension per level
3. Position as "the tool MOET could adopt" when they finalize criteria

### HCMC Is the Beachhead
HCMC consistently sets higher targets than national requirements (30% vs 20% Level 1 for K-12 by 2030). Their De An 5695 already proved CLIL works. HCMC schools are the most prepared and most motivated.

### May 14, 2026 Deadline Is the Forcing Function
Preschools must register for EMI pilot by May 14, 2026. This creates immediate demand for readiness assessment and onboarding tools.

### Technology & AI Is Explicitly Mandated
Dimension #4 of the evaluation framework is "Digital transformation, AI application, and physical infrastructure." AI is not optional — it's a scored criterion. Learneris's ai-gateway is directly aligned.

# Deep Policy Research — EMI Level Framework

## Key Finding

**The detailed rubric defining Level 1/2/3 criteria (the Phụ lục/Annex of QD 2371) is NOT publicly available as of April 2026.** The implementing thông tư has not been issued. HCMC has stated criteria are still being drafted.

This confirms Learneris's opportunity to define the de facto assessment standard.

## Who Is Building the Official Criteria

**ACER UK + British Council + VNIES + NFLP Expert Working Group** is crafting policy recommendations for MOET:
- Partners: VNIES, NFLP, Hanoi Pedagogical University 2, ULIS, British Council Vietnam
- Three focus areas: teacher quality, student attainment, EMI feasibility
- Recommendations submitted to MOET in early 2025
- Source: [acer.org](https://www.acer.org/my/news/article/transforming-english-language-education-in-vietnam-a-collaborative-path-forward)

**British Council–VNIES TPD Framework (March–August 2026):**
- Developing Teacher Professional Development Framework for MOET's EMI strategy
- Source: [britishcouncil.vn](https://www.britishcouncil.vn/en/teach/call-proposals-uk-viet-nam-research-grants)

## Singapore Model — Explicitly Referenced but Rejected

British Council Vietnam (2024): "When implementing the policy of English as a second language... English should not be considered a universal 'language of instruction' like Singapore if there are not enough necessary resources; selective bilingualism can be applied to high-quality schools."

Vietnam is "Expanding Circle" (unlike Singapore/Malaysia "Outer Circle"). The Vietnam approach: English as "very special, top-priority foreign language" — not a national language.

# EMI Go-to-Market Playbook

## Strategy: "Regulatory Wedge + Platform Lock-in"

Learneris's GTM strategy leverages its prepared school onboarding kit and existing government certifications to become the default EMI technology partner. The playbook:

1. **Free tool** (EMI Readiness Assessment) gets schools on the platform
2. **Standardized document kit** makes Learneris the path of least resistance for EMI approval
3. **Teacher training** creates qualified users who depend on the platform
4. **Content engine** makes switching cost prohibitive
5. **Dashboard/reporting** embeds Learneris in the DoE workflow

## Immediate Actions (April-May 2026)

### Hanoi Track
- Deploy Standardized Kit to 50+ schools through existing Vietlearn/AVS partnership
- Target: 20 schools submit EMI pilot applications naming Learneris
- KPI: # of schools with approved EMI pilots using Learneris

### HCMC Track
- The May 14, 2026 deadline (SGDDT-GDMN 2551) is the forcing function
- Schools must register for AY 2026-2027 preschool English pilot
- Action: Offer free EMI Readiness Assessment to HCMC preschools
- Leverage KDI network (180+ Makerspaces, 100K+ students) for warm introductions
- Target existing school relationships (Nguyen Sieu model -> referrals)

### National Track
- KH 18/KH-BGDDT teacher survey runs Jan-Jun 2026
- Position Learneris assessment tools as complementary to the national survey
- Opportunity: if MOET needs digital tools for the survey, be the vendor

## Pricing Model

### For Schools (B2B)
| Tier | What's Included | Price Model |
|------|----------------|-------------|
| Free | EMI Readiness Assessment, basic diagnostic | Free forever |
| Starter | Assessment + Teacher Companion App + 50 CLIL lessons | Per-teacher/year |
| Professional | Full platform + unlimited CLIL content + PD training | Per-school/year |
| Enterprise | Multi-school dashboard + DoE reporting + custom content | Per-district/year |

### For Departments of Education (B2G)
- City/province-wide license
- Includes administrative dashboard and reporting
- Custom branding option
- Direct contract with So GD&DT

### For Department of Education
"Learneris provides the monitoring, reporting, and quality assurance tools the Department needs to track EMI implementation across all schools in the province."

### For Teachers
"You don't need perfect English to teach with EMI. Learneris gives you real-time support, bilingual lesson plans, and a pronunciation guide so you can confidently integrate English into your subjects."

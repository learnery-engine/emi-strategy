🤖 AI Math Tutor

How do I solve 2x + 3 = 7?
Great question! Let's solve step by step:

Step 1: Subtract 3 from both sides
2x + 3 - 3 = 7 - 3
2x = 4

Step 2: Divide both sides by 2
x = 2 ✓
